### Key Concepts to Remember:
- 3 ACSM Risk Modulators: current activity level, disease/symptoms, desired exercise intensity
- ACSM Algorithm: 2 pathways (exercising vs not) × 3 conditions (no disease, known disease, symptoms)
- 3-Step Checklist: Step 1 Symptoms → STOP if yes; Step 2 Activity level; Step 3 Medical conditions
- PAR-Q+: valid 12 months; YES to follow-up → ePARmed-X+ at eparmedx.com
- Beta Blockers: blunt HR — use RPE or talk test, NOT target HR
- Diuretics: dehydration + electrolyte imbalance → arrhythmia risk
