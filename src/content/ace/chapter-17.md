This chapter is a complete **anatomy and human movement** reference — the foundation everything else in personal training is built on.

It covers how the human movement system produces motion: the three **planes of motion** and their axes, **joint actions**, **muscle actions** (isolated vs. integrated), the four **muscle roles**, **length-tension** relationships, **force-couples**, and **levers** — followed by a full **origin / insertion / function / innervation** reference for every major muscle, organized region by region with anatomical images.
