Welcome to **Chapter 1: Role & Scope of Practice for Personal Trainers**.

### Key Concepts to Remember:
- ACE CPT assesses **minimum competency** — the threshold to work safely with minimal supervision
- **Scope of practice** = legal range of services; shaped by education, certification, and state laws
- **NCCA accreditation** = gold standard; places ACE on par with nursing, dietetics, athletic training
- **USREPS** = 175,000+ registered exercise professionals; maintained by CREP
- **Outside scope**: diagnosing illness, prescribing diets/supplements, treating injury, massage without license
- **Within scope**: exercise programming, health screening, referral, sharing MyPlate/Dietary Guidelines
- **HIPAA**: client health info is confidential; written permission required before sharing with physician
- **ACE renewal**: 2-year cycle, minimum 20 ACE-approved CECs including CPR/AED
